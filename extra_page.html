Dr Peter Malakar
Passion Movement International was founded in Delhi, India by medical Dr. Peter and Heather Malakar with a vision to develop and challenge potential leaders around the world for a life of integrity, hope and compassionate service. PMI brings people together from different places to share expertise and resources, while developing friendships and seeking new opportunities to serve together for local and global impact.
